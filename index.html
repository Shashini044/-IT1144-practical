Traveler guidelines

  • Respect Temples: Dress modestly (shoulders and knees covered).
  • Currency: Carry local rupees (LKR) for rural excursions.
  • Optimal Seasons: December to April for South beaches.
  • Environmental Duty: Maintain a strict zero-litter policy.
